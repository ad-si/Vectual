What is vectual?

vectual is a powerful tool that helps you to visualize data. Display your data as beautiful pie-, bar-, line-, scattercharts or even as tagclouds and in maps! And if that wasn't enough vectual is real open source software and solely based on the most modern web standards like HTML5 and SVG!

Good Looking

Display beautiful charts and customise them to fit perfectly to your needs.

Modern

In contrast to other charting libraries, which are build upon flash, vectual is solely based on the newest web standards! So even your cell phones and your tablets are able to display the beautiful charts!

Open Source

The whole Library will get released as Open Source Software. So you will be able to use it for all of your projects - both non-profit and commercial!

Technology

A PHP library is dynamically generating SVG Images. The fancy animations are implemented through SMIL. At the moment it's working without any Javascript and I try to avoid it as much as possible, though I will most likely have to implement certain parts with javascript as the functionality of SMIL isn't fully developed yet.
